About me

"Hello everyone, I'm Janna Conda, currently immersed in the vibrant world of BSIT studies at Sorsogon State University. Beyond the realm of technology and algorithms, I find solace and joy in the art of singing. It's not merely a pastime; it's a profound expression of my innermost self. Ever since I can remember, music has been an integral part of my life. From humming tunes as a child to performing in local talent shows, every note resonates deeply within me. Singing isn't just about hitting the right pitch; it's about pouring my emotions into every lyric and melody, creating a connection that transcends words. In the midst of debugging code or analyzing algorithms, music serves as my sanctuary, offering a refuge from the complexities of the digital realm. It's where I find balance, where creativity flows freely, and where I discover new perspectives. As I navigate through the intricacies of my BSIT journey, I carry with me the harmonies and rhythms that fuel my passion. Whether I'm coding lines of software or belting out melodies, I approach each endeavor with the same fervor and dedication. In essence, I'm a BSIT student with a heart full of music, constantly seeking harmony between the binary world of technology and the soul-stirring melodies of song."
